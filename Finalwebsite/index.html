Welcome to Point Break Surfing.

We are passionate commonity of wave catchers dedicate to share the joy of surfing in one of the best destinatons to surf: Fuerteventura. Beautiful landscabe and turquoise waters of the Canary Islands, this company is here for the love of ocean.

Our journey began with a group of friend who had a common passion for surfing and sharing that love to others. We combined our expertise in the sport and started to create our own surf school that is more than just surfing school. We wanted to create a community for our students. At Break Point Surfing we belive that surfing is so much more than just a sport, it is a way of life. Embracing ocean and feeling freedom inside of the rythm of the ocean that never ends. We offer classes to everyone who is even slighlty intrested to already more excperienced wave catchers.

Discover Fuertoventura

Fuerteventura, nestled in the Canary Islands, beckons with its legendary surf spots, sun-kissed beaches, and breathtaking landscapes. Welcoming tens of thousands of residents and travelers annually, this island paradise offers an array of activities, from thrilling water sports to leisurely hikes. With its rich history, vibrant culture, and laid-back charm, Fuerteventura promises an unforgettable blend of relaxation, exploration, and the exhilaration of riding the waves.
